About

Welcome to the Napoleonic Wars, an epoch of profound historical significance that left an indelible mark on our understanding of military strategy. Napoleon is a captivating and intriguing character from whom we can learn a lot. And it is my hope that you will discover, as I have, the allure of his story. To facilitate curiosity, a curated selection of videos and books awaits you in the Resources section for further discovery about Napoleon and his era. This website endeavor is to present an accurate depiction of events. Should you wish to contribute to this pursuit of accuracy, I welcome you to offer you feedback through the submission form. Much of the text is generated with the help of AI. I have tried to control the accuracy, however there is not a gurantee! For this reason I engourage you to do your own research and to not refer to this website in any scientific writings or schoolwork. Now go to the timeline to learn more!
May your reading be enjoyable.
